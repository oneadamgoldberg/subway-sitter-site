Good to know

What is it made of?

A soft, lightweight nonwoven fabric (the same family used for surgical drapes), with a mild, residue-free adhesive on the edges. Latex-free.

Will it leave marks on the seat?

No. The adhesive is low-tack and repositionable — it holds through your ride and peels away clean, leaving nothing behind.

Does it really fit every seat?

It's one continuous sheet that covers the seatback and the seat, and the adhesive grips hard plastic, vinyl and fabric alike.
